## Project Structure  
  
Frequency of Life uses an **open-core governance model**.  
  
- Open-source components are community-driven  
- Core intellectual property remains proprietary  
- Final decision authority rests with maintainers  
  
---  
  
## Roles  
  
### Maintainers  
- Approve PRs  
- Set technical direction  
- Manage releases  
- Protect IP boundaries  
  
### Contributors  
- Submit PRs to open repos  
- Participate in discussions  
- Earn trust and responsibility over time  
  
### Community Members  
- Provide feedback  
- Participate in beta testing  
- Suggest improvements  
  
---  
  
## Decision-Making  
  
- Minor changes: maintainer discretion  
- Major changes: discussion-based consensus  
- Core IP decisions: reserved exclusively to Frequency of Life ownership  
  
---  
  
## Intellectual Property  
  
- Contributions apply **only** to open-source code  
- No contributor gains rights to proprietary systems  
- All contributions fall under the CLA  
  
---  
  
## Transparency  
  
Roadmaps and decisions are communicated publicly where appropriate, without exposing proprietary logic.
