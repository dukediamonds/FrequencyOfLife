# Code of Conduct  
  
This project follows the Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct.  
  
---  
  
## Our Pledge  
  
We are committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free experience for everyone.  
  
---  
  
## Our Standards  
  
Examples of positive behavior:  
- Respectful communication  
- Constructive feedback  
- Empathy and openness  
  
Unacceptable behavior:  
- Harassment or discrimination  
- Personal attacks  
- Publishing private information  
- Deliberate boundary violations  
  
---  
  
## Enforcement  
  
Project maintainers are responsible for enforcement.  
Violations may result in warnings, PR rejection, or bans.  
  
---  
  
## Reporting  
  
Report issues to:  
security@frequencyoflife.app (or designated contact)  
  
---  
  
This Code of Conduct is adapted from the Contributor Covenant, version 2.1.
